What is a QR Code?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information, such as website URLs, plain text, email addresses, and phone numbers. Unlike traditional barcodes, QR codes can be easily scanned using modern smartphone cameras, instantly connecting the physical world to digital content.

Why Use Our Free QR Code Generator?

Our tool is built for speed and privacy. The generation happens in real-time directly in your browser. This means your private data (like Wi-Fi passwords or vCard contact details) is never sent to our servers. It is 100% free, fast, and secure.
